Our Story

A Family Rooted in Uganda
Est.
1914

Christopher Kitalimusango Magoye & Deborah Nalumansi, c. 1921

The Magoye family traces its roots to Uganda, where Christopher Kitalimusango Magoye, son of Amos Lule Nkunga, became the family pioneer. Christopher married Deborah Nalumansi, and together they raised 14 children, each of whom has continued the family legacy with their own children and stories.

Christopher and Deborah's devotion and resilience laid the foundation for generations of Magoye descendants. Today, the Magoye family continues to grow, united by love, tradition, and a shared heritage.
